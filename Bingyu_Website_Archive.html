About Me

I am Bingyu, a freelance designer and full-time data scientist based in Boston. Originally from Suzhou, Jiangsu, China, I channel my passion for art through both hand-drawn and digital mediums.

My artistic style, ranging from sci-fi to Eastern classic, is a testament to my deep connection to cultural heritage, creative imagination, and analytic background.
